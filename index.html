DroptheBeat

Drop the Beat

Based on the Wintergatan music machine, this device drops marbles onto pipes to play music. It plays notes by feeding marbles into a laser-cut song sheet with marble-sized holes representing notes. The sheet is then slid over an opening, causing the marbles to fall out and hit their corresponding pipes below, playing a beautiful melody.
